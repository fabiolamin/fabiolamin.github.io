The Hourglass Tale


Project: Team

Game engine: Unity

Platform: PC

The Hourglass Tale is a 3D platform game that the player has a skill to stop the time. The game was made for Game Dev Jam and was one of the best three games evaluated by players, in addition The Hourglass Tale audio was nominated as the best.

The team was composed by 2 programmers, 1 level designer, 1 artist and 1 creative person.

My role as programmer was:

  • Implement the enemy’s movement divided by hunter enemies, patrolling enemies and flying enemies.
  • Implement the boss behavior
  • Implement the audio manager
